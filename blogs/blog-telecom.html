Telecommunication Operations

Date: 2 Dec 2020

Telecom operations involve managing BTS towers, optical fiber networks, microwave links, and large-scale telecom infrastructure. ECE engineers play a key role in ensuring network reliability and optimizing communication systems.

Typical Roles

  • Telecom Technician
  • OFC (Optical Fiber Cable) Engineer
  • BSS/RF Operations Engineer

Industries & Opportunities

Telecom is one of the largest employers for ECE graduates, offering opportunities in both field operations and network planning. Engineers can work with telecom operators, government projects, or private infrastructure companies.

Future Trends

With 5G, IoT, and smart cities on the rise, telecom operations are becoming more complex and critical. Engineers who understand both hardware and software aspects of networks will be in high demand.
